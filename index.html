About Me

I am a 26 year-old graphic designer, originally from Mexico. I graduated from Texas A&M University with a Bachelor of Science in Visualization with a minor in math. My passions are graphic design and photography. I am very expressive, motivated, creative and open minded. My aspiration is to one day become an acclaimed graphic designer with fresh, innovative, fun and eye-catching designs throughout the world.
